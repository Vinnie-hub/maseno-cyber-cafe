03 — Who we are

Straight talk, no confusion

We are a local cyber café in Maseno — not Maseno University, and not affiliated with it in any way. Everything official (your admission status, fee structure, and official joining instructions) comes only from the university itself. We simply help you prepare, print, and apply.

HELB is free

Applying for HELB costs nothing on the official HELB portal. We only charge for our time helping you use it.

We don't hold your money

We never ask you to send fee payments through us. All university fees go directly to Maseno University's official accounts.

Ask us anything

If you're unsure whether something is official, ask us — we'd rather tell you than have you guess.

04 — FAQ

Common questions

Is this the official Maseno University site?
No. We're an independent, privately run cyber café in Maseno. For anything official — admission status, fee balances, deadlines — always confirm directly with the university.
Do I pay HELB application fees to you?
No. Applying for HELB is free on the official HELB portal. We only charge for the time we spend helping you fill it in — never for the application itself.
Can I pay my university fees through you?
No. We never handle fee payments. All university fees should go directly to Maseno University's official accounts.
What should I bring with me?
Your admission letter, national ID, and any documents you already have printed or saved on your phone/email.
